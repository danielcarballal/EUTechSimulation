Welcome to the European Horizons Simulation

Created by Daniel Carballal, University of Illinois Urbana Champaign

The twenty first century has come -- and what a world we live in! We can send more information from New York, USA to York, England in seconds than we could in months a century ago. But this is not without growing pains, as all growth brings conflict with it. So, today, you will become one of two parties- the European Union or Silicon Valley tech companies. Will you be able to deal with conflict in order to grow the 21st century world we are destined to live in? Choose which team you want to be a part of.
